How to Make the Perfect Suqaar

Ingredients

  1. 2 kilo diced lamb meat - for 1 kilo double all the ingredients. (up to 6 garlic cloves) we use leg of lamb. I just ask the butcher to cut it up for me into small cubes.

  2. 1 large onion

  3. 1 large red pepper or mixed peppers

  4. 4 cloves garlic

  5. 1 large tomato

  6. 1 handful coriander

  7. 1 tablespoon garam masala

  8. 2 tablespoons tamarind flesh. If using tamarind from a jar then 1 heaped tablespoon is perfect.

  9. 1 tablespoon roasted coriander seed powder. Definitely not the same thing as coriander powder. I roasted the seeds first THEN blended. Big and bold flavour!!

  10. Generous pinch of salt to your preference

  11. Half a lemon


Instructions

  1. 1.First start off by prepping. Blend together the tomato, 3 garlic cloves, and the coriander. Add a tiny bit of water if needed to blitz up into a semi-smooth mixture. Chop the onion, peppers, and 1 garlic clove.
  2. 2. On a medium hot pan, sauté the onions, pepper, and 1 garlic clove. Add in the spices and mix.
  3. 3. After a few mixes, add in the lamb. Immediately add salt, stir, and let the meat cook with a lid on for a couple of minutes.
  4. 4. At this point, stir in the tamarind. Mix again and add the lid on for a further 7 or so minutes.
  5. 5. The lamb should be starting to brown nicely and caramelize almost. Now add in the tomato mixture. Lid on for 5 minutes or so to allow the tomato mixture to cook down and soak into the caramelized lamb.
  6. 6. Squeeze with some lemon, top with extra coriander, and you are done❤️.
